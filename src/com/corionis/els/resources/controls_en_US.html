Navigation:  
• Alt-letter and Alt-number combinations select menus and tabs.
• Tab, Shift-Tab, Ctrl-Tab, and Ctrl-Shift-Tab navigate panels.
• Editing text fields requires Enter or Tab to complete the change.
• Saving a dialog also saves its position, size, etc. Cancelling does not.
